HLNFit Design Notes

Main Goal: Allow analysis of raw data.
Main Parameters: 
    1. FileName with the raw data
    2. Type of the function to fit: Full or Truncated
    3. Initial values of the parameters
    4. Maximum field
    5. Whether to consider normal B^2 background?
    
    
Main Results:
    1. alpha and Bphi or B1, B2, B3, B4 and their interpretation
    2. Graph with the quality of the fit -- a brunch and an the whole fit
